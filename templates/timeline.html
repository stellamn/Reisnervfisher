Ferbruary 2, 1917

Fisher writes to Reisner about misunderstandings in the allocation of men. Obvious tension on the subject of employees building.

May 14, 1917

Fisher writes to Reisner and asks him to come to the dahabiyeh “in order to talk over very serious matters.”

June 8, 1917

Reisner to Fisher with details on how Fisher’s hiring members of the Firnisis family have caused lots of prestige problems at Reisner’s expedition. Reisner can’t come to town and meet with Fisher because of work but is willing to entertain Fisher at his camp.

June 26, 1917

Apparently unsent letter from Reisner to Gordon detailing the very complex relationships between the working men and the reasons he is unable to dismiss Mahmud.

January 9, 1918

Reisner mails his statement with an explanatory letter to Dr. Gordon at the U. of P. as promised and “I leave the matter to your judgment.”

June 20, 1918

Reisner writes to Fisher a very polite letter that has nothing to do with their conflict. There will be no more letters on the subject, at least in this file.
